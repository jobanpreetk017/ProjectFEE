About RoomCraft

RoomCraft is a creative project by first-year students of Chitkara University. It allows users to design their dream room with drag-and-drop furniture, floor customization, and interactive features. Our goal is to combine design and technology to make room planning fun and engaging.

✨ What you can do:
✔ Drag any furniture into your room
✔ Reposition items by dragging
✔ Double-click to resize
✔ Click furniture then press Delete to remove
✔ Change floor color instantly
